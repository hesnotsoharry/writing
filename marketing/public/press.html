About Writers Nook

Writers Nook is a local-first desktop writing app built for novelists. It brings together a distraction-free editor, a binder for organizing your manuscript, a story bible for tracking characters and places, and a corkboard for seeing the shape of your story — all in one calm, paper-warm room. Your writing stays on your own machine, in plain files you always own. Writers Nook is a one-time purchase, built and maintained independently in Canada.
