What's new

Small, careful improvements. Here's what's changed in Writers Nook.

Version 1.0

Latest June 2026
  • The first public release of Writers Nook.
  • Distraction-free editor, binder, story bible, and corkboard.
  • Daily goals, gentle streaks, and light & dark "Quiet Study" themes.
  • Export to Markdown, DOCX, and print-ready PDF.
  • Free off-site backup via Dropbox or OneDrive — point the app at your own folder, no subscription needed.

Version 0.9

Beta May 2026
  • Release-candidate polish across all views.
  • Faster project switching and search.
  • Refined the page-turn motion between scenes.

Version 0.8

Beta April 2026
  • Introduced the full-entry story-bible screen.
  • Added per-scene status and word counts on the corkboard.
  • Early local backup plumbing (save a copy of your library anywhere on your device).
