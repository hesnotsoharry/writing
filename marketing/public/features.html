Goals & streaks

Encouragement, never pressure

Set a daily word goal and Writers Nook keeps a quiet ring filling through the day. Show up two days running and a gentle streak appears. Miss a day? Nothing scolds you — the ring is simply there again tomorrow, patient as ever.

  • Live counts for the scene, the chapter, and the whole book
