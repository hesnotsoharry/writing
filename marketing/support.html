How do I activate my license?

Open Writers Nook, choose "Enter license key," and paste the key from your purchase email or your account. It activates on up to three of your computers.

I lost my license key — what now?

Sign in with the email you bought with and your key (and downloads) will be right there. The same link restores everything on a new computer.

Is cloud backup required?

No. The app is fully featured offline. Backup & Sync is an optional $5/mo add-on for off-site copies and syncing across devices.
