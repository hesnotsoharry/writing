06PrinciplesWhat "calm" means in practice.
1
The page is the hero.

Chrome is quiet parchment; the canvas is the brightest surface. Generous margins, a 38rem measure, serif body. Nothing competes with the words.

2
Everything in reach, nothing shouting.

Binder left, inspector right, both dismissible. Actions are icon-quiet until hovered. One restrained accent carries selection, primary action, and goal progress.

3
Forgiving by default.

Goals are off until asked for. Deleting a chapter never loses prose. Quick capture never loses your place. Backup status sits calm in the status bar.

4
Warm, not clinical.

Paper whites over pure white, ink over black, clay over blue. A literary tool should feel like a good notebook, not a control panel.
