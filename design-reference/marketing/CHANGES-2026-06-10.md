**2e. Corkboard section → "Corkboard & outliner"** (headline unchanged)
- Eyebrow: `The corkboard` → `Corkboard &amp; outliner`
- Paragraph:

> NEW: Zoom out and your scenes become index cards on a board — synopsis on the front, status colour at a glance. Drag them into a new order to fix the pacing, and the manuscript follows along. Prefer rows to cards? Flip the same view into the outliner: a sortable table of every scene's status, synopsis, word count, and labels. Cards for shuffling, the outline for hierarchy — one structure, two ways to see it.

- Checklist (was 3 bullets, now 4):
  1. Reorder scenes by dragging cards
  2. Flip to the outliner — sort by status, words, or label, edit inline
  3. Colour labels you define, shared across both views
  4. Click any card or row to drop straight into the scene
